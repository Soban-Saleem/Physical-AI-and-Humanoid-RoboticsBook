## Educational Philosophy

Our team believes in **democratizing access to cutting-edge technology education**. This philosophy drives our approach to making complex topics accessible while maintaining technical rigor and practical relevance.

### Core Principles

1. **Learning by Building**: Students learn best through hands-on implementation
2. **Industry Relevance**: Curriculum reflects real-world industry practices
3. **Open Source**: Knowledge should be openly shared and accessible
4. **Continuous Innovation**: Curriculum evolves with technological advances
5. **Global Accessibility**: Education should transcend geographical boundaries

## Research Interests

The team's research spans several areas of Physical AI:

- **Embodied Intelligence**: How physical form shapes cognitive capabilities
- **Human-Robot Interaction**: Natural and intuitive robot communication
- **Educational Technology**: Innovative approaches to technical education
- **Open Source Robotics**: Community-driven development of robotics tools
- **AI Safety**: Ensuring safe deployment of Physical AI systems
