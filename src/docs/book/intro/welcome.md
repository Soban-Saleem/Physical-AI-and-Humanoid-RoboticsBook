### What is Physical AI?

**Physical AI** represents the next evolution of artificial intelligence - AI systems that don't just think, but also act in the physical world. Unlike traditional AI confined to digital spaces, Physical AI:

- **Understands physical laws** (gravity, friction, momentum)
- **Operates in 3D space** with real-world constraints
- **Interacts with objects** through manipulation and navigation
- **Adapts to dynamic environments** with uncertainty
